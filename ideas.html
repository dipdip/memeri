How do we get everyone to live comfortably and be successful? Certainly, each person has their own idea of success. More often than not, their fantasy idea of success is to have everything without ever working for it.

The lotto is what everyone turns to for their chance at success. What happens when you win the lotto. You can buy everything you want. You don't have to worry about rent. You have a sense of freedom to do whatever you like. You can pursue your dreams, and if you fail, you can just start all over again without financial worries.

Clearly there are advantages with being financially independent. Are the majority of us in such a dire situation that we cannot hope to accomplish anything outside of work? I certainly don't think so. In today's society, many advances we've made have enabled the majority of us to accomplish the same things without needing to go through the tedious motions. What used to require the highest IQ to solve can now be done by simply entering a few numbers into a spreadsheet. So what does this mean? I think it means that humans are evolving in a way to enable all of us to accomplish common things without needing to repeat what many of us have painstakingly done. So the pioneers have worked hard for a better life for the future. Does that mean we should just sit back and wait for everything around us to be done? Perhaps. I guess then it really comes down to what we are living for. It would seem that a lot of what our great achievers have enabled us to do is to free up our time for better understanding of the universe and to a certain extent, allowing us to manage our relationships. Never before were we able to have so much information at our fingertips. There is very little physical barriers left between a human being and knowledge. If you have an idea, you can look it up, and find investors online to back it up. Yet, only a small fraction of us do anything with this. We would rather sit and complain about our jobs, complain about the weather, stress over relationships and wish that we win the lotto.

So back to our question. How do we get everyone to live comfortably and be successful? Maybe the majority of us already have that freedom, but we focus on the wrong things.
